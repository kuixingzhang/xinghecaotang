<p>
不堪回首的青春，是埋藏在心底的愛戀。
</p>

<p>
初次相見時<br>
你燦爛的笑容<br>
如天使輕輕拂過<br>
我內心深處的朦朧<br>
心動的感覺<br>
如同這小小的時鐘<br>
包裹著靈魂的激盪<br>
是永不停歇的滴嗒聲<br>
計算著時光的分秒<br>
每一刻都像在傾訴中
</p>

<p>
但這樣的美好敵不過<br>
命運齒輪的猙獰<br>
到了暫時的避風港<br>
卻駛不到愛的歸程<br>
歲月飛快流逝<br>
當我們已不再年輕<br>
愛戀的感動不會消滅<br>
一切都變得波瀾不驚<br>
握著這時鐘恰似光陰靜止<br>
凝固在會心的永恆
</p>

<p><em>寫於 2024年5月27日</em></p>

<h3>The Little Clock</h3>

<div class="english-preface">

Some loves are never truly lost. They simply retreat into the quietest chambers of the heart, where memory grows gentler with the passing years. Youth may fade, dreams may surrender to time, yet somewhere a small clock still keeps the rhythm of a heart that once learned, for the very first time, how to love.
</div>

<div class="english-poem">
The day I first beheld your smile,<br>
the world grew hushed for just a while.<br>
An angel's touch, so soft, so bright,<br>
awoke my hidden heart to light.<br>
A little clock began to chime,<br>
not keeping hours—but keeping time;<br>
each quiet beat, both sweet and true,<br>
counted every dream of you.
<br><br>
But love could never outrun fate,<br>
whose turning wheels would never wait.<br>
We found a harbor, calm and warm,<br>
yet missed the home beyond the storm.<br>
The years moved on with silent grace;<br>
youth slipped away without a trace.<br>
Yet first love's fire still burns the same,<br>
a gentle, everlasting flame.
<br><br>
Whenever this small clock I hold,<br>
its patient hands refuse to grow old.<br>
The ticking slows, the world grows still;<br>
time lingers, simply by its will.<br>
One smile remains, serene and true,<br>
forever bright with morning dew;<br>
not lost in years that come and flee,<br>
but living where time forgets to be.
</div>
