鄉愁是捨不得卻無法停留  
鄉愁是征途上頻頻的回眸  
鄉愁是浮華裡固執的守候  
鄉愁是孤寂中似水的溫柔  

![鄉愁](/assets/images/poetry/xiangchou.jpg)

<h3>Homesickness</h3>

<div class="english-poem">

Homesickness is longing to stay when it's time to depart.<br>
Homesickness is turning around with an unfinished heart.<br>

Homesickness is keeping faith while passing splendors fade.<br>
Homesickness is water-soft grace that lingers in the shade.

</div>
