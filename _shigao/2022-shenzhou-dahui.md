神州無處不維新，四十年來夢想真。  
但見英雄排座次，孰憐百姓濟斯民。  
天將大任毋多讓，四海歸心敢不臣。  
舊日朝堂名利客，惟聞謳歌頌皇秦。  

*寫於 2022年10月25日*

<h3>Reflections on a National Congress</h3>
<div class="english-poem">

The banners changed, yet promised brighter days;<br>
Old dreams returned beneath triumphant praise.<br>

The mighty gathered, counting rank and throne;<br>
The voiceless waited, suffering alone.<br>

When power calls itself the nation's voice,<br>
Who dares refuse, or claims another choice?<br>

The court still sings beneath the victor's crown;<br>
While every honest voice is slowly drowned.

</div>
